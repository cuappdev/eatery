Looking for somewhere to eat around Cornell's campus? Eatery has your back.

See what's open on campus, browse menus of dining locations, search for your favorite foods, check your dining account balance, and so much more!

Let us know what you think! Send us feedback or give us ideas for new features by tweeting @cornellappdev

Eatery is the first app from CUAppDev, an open source iOS project team at Cornell University. Check us out at cuappdev.org or contribute at www.github.com/cuappdev/eatery
